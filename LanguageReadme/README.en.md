## Section 3: Turn on the Microphone, Enable Wake-up, and Turn off the Speaker

![Lecture](<./images/course2/section-03-01.png>)

Once Fay has started, the next critical step is to ensure that the microphone is turned on, the speaker is turned off, and the wake-up feature is enabled. 

The UE digital human model demonstrated today does not have an independent audio capture module and relies on the local machine's Fay for audio input. However, it does have an independent audio playback module, which will play the audio synthesized by Fay. This audio still needs to be played on the same machine.

If the wake-up feature of Fay is turned off and you do not have professional audio capture hardware, Fay might pick up the sound emitted by the digital human itself, causing an echo loop that severely impacts the conversation experience.

After configuration, you can try speaking into the microphone and say, Hello,areyouthere? to verify if Fay can correctly receive your voice.

## Section 11: Verify Connection and Conduct a Conversation Test

![Lecture](<./images/course2/section-11-01.png>)

The final step is to verify whether the connection between the UE Digital Human and Fay has been successfully established and to conduct a complete conversation test.

After the UE Digital Human starts, return to the Fay management interface to check the connection status. At the top-left corner near the Fay logo, you will see several status indicators. If the second indicator light is on, it means the UE Digital Human has successfully connected to the Fay service, and the two-way communication link has been fully established.

Once the connection is confirmed, you can greet the digital human through the microphone. Try saying, Hello,pleaseintroduceyourself, and observe the digital human's response.

If you see the digital human's mouth and facial expressions change and hear a clear voice response from the UE Digital Human's running window, congratulations! You have successfully completed the setup and integration of the UE Digital Human.

At this point, the entire setup process for the UE Digital Human is complete. You now have a digital human companion that can listen, speak, and interact. You can start exploring more interesting application scenarios.
